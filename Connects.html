Field Force Automation (FFA) is a service platform for supporting the diagnosis and solution of service events. It integrates with multiple sources into streamlined workflows, allowing you to analyze problems and provide solutions faster to business users.

FFA application is used to fix customer issues remotely whenever possible. When a remote fix is not possible, it is delivered to Field Engineers so they are ready to resolve the customer's issue efficiently.
