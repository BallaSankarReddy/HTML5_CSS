Field Force Automation (FFA) is a service platform for supporting the diagnosis and solution of service events. It integrating with multiple sources into streamlined workflows, allowing you to analyze problems and provide solutions faster to business users.

FFA application usage to fix the customer issues remotely wherever possible .When remotely fix is not possible, it deliver to Fes so they are ready to resolve the customer issue efficiently
